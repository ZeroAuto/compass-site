What is OkGamer

OkGamer is designed to connect players on online multiplayer games like Grand Theft Auto V with other games that share their gaming personality. It began as a final project at MakerSquare that two other developers and myself built in Ruby on Rails over the course of three weeks.

  • This is the splash page that a user sees before they log in
  • After a user signs up they give their Xbox Live Gamertag and answer a short quiz
  • At which point they are matched with other users based on their answers to the above quiz
  • A user can then send a message to his or her matches to set up team formation
